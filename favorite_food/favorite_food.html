How to make Onion Rings

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk, or as much as you need
  • 3/4 cups dry bread crumbs
  • More salt for taste
  • 4 cups ofoil, or as much as you want

Directions

  1. Cut the onion into slices. Seperate the slices into rings. Set them aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. [OPTIONAL]Cover the onion rings in the mixture, and set aside on a plate
  4. Whisk the egg, and pour the egg and the milk into the flour mixture. Stir it until it forms a consistent batter.
  5. Dip the onion rings into the batter, and set it aside on a wire rack until it stops dripping.
  6. Put the bread crumbs into a plate or bowl. One at a time, put the onion rings into the bread crumbs and cover the battered onion rings with bread crumbs until they are completely covered.
  7. Pour the oil into a pot, and heat it to 185 degrees C.
  8. Deep fry the onion rings until they are golden brown, then put on paper towels to drain the excess oil.
  9. Season with salt and ketchup if you want. Enjoy!
